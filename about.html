Poor communication gets in the way...

In the summer of 2021, we got one of our websites redesigned. When we interviewed the target SME, one point clearly resonated at the release meeting - "These guys just cannot communicate." Throughout our discussions, we could articulate only in mind and we wish to think we understood.

We spent time over and after explaining our requirements clearly, when we were suggested to get the full package which would cost equivalent to a yearly pay.

It wasn't the first time...

This experience was not the first for us - a few years earlier, we had partnered with another software development company for one of our products. The entire engagement was challenging from the start and we found ourselves in endless discussions regarding the design due to its lack of communication skills.
